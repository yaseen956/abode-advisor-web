Frequently Asked Questions

How can I list my property?

You can list your property by creating an account and using our property listing form. Our team will verify and publish your listing within 24 hours.

Is there any fee for searching properties?

No, searching and browsing properties on Property99 is completely free. We only charge a small commission when a transaction is completed.

How do I contact a property owner?

You can contact property owners directly through our platform using the contact forms or phone numbers provided in the property listings.

Yes, we have partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions to help you get the best home loan deals at competitive rates.
